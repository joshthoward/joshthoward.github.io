About

iidblog is a play on words. For the mathematics and statistics communities, iid is an initialism which stands for independent and identically distributed and describes a desirable property of random variables for machine learning. For this blog, iid describes the sentiment of my take at yet another professional blog on machine learning.

I hope you enjoy.
